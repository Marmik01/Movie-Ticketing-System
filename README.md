## Project Overview
This project is a movie booking system that allows users to browse available movies, book tickets, and manage their bookings. Admins can manage users, movies, shows, and bookings.

## 📌 Admin Navigation Guide

### 🔑 1. Login as Admin  
- Click **"Log In"** at the top-right corner  
- Enter **Admin Credentials**:  
  - **Email/Username:** `abc`  
  - **Password:** `password123`  
- Click **"Log In"** to access the Admin Dashboard  



### 👤 2. Manage Profile  
- Click **"Edit Profile"** at the top  
- **Note:** Admin **cannot change username, ID, or password**  



### 👥 3. Manage Users (View, Create, Edit, Delete)  
- Click **"Users List"** to see all registered users  
- Click **"New User"** to **add** a user  
- Click **"Show This User"** to **edit or delete** a user  



### 🎥 4. Manage Movies (Create, View, Edit, Delete)  
- Click **"Home"** (Top Navigation)  
- Under each **movie name**, you can:  
  - 🎬 **View Details** → Click **"View"**  
  - ✏️ **Edit** → Click **"Edit"**  
  - 🗑️ **Delete** → Click **"Delete"**  
  - ➕ **Add a new movie** → Click **"New Movie"**  



### 🎭 5. Manage Shows (Create, View, Edit, Delete) for a Specific Movie  
- Click **"Home"** → Click **"View"** under a movie name  
- Click **"View Shows"** to see all scheduled shows  
- Actions available:  
  - 🎟 **Book a Show** → Click **"Book Show"**  
  - ➕ **Add a Show** → Click **"New Show"**  
  - ✏️ **Edit a Show** → Click **"Edit Show"**  
  - 🗑️ **Delete a Show** → Click **"Delete Show"**  



### 🎟 6. Manage Tickets (Create, View, Edit, Cancel, Delete)  
- Click **"Home"** → Click **"View"** under a movie → Click **"View Shows"**  
- Click **"Book Show"** to purchase a ticket  
- Click **"All Booked Tickets"** to view all booked tickets  
- Actions available:  
  - ✏️ **Edit** a ticket  
  - ❌ **Cancel** a ticket  
  - 🗑️ **Delete** a ticket

## 📌 User Navigation Guide

### 🔑 1. **Sign Up / Log In**  
- Click **"Sign Up"** at the top  
- Enter your details  
- Click **"Sign Up"** to create your account

- If you already have an account, click **"Log In"**  
- Enter **Username/Email** and **Password**  
- Click **"Log In"** to access your User Dashboard  



### 👤 2. **Edit Your Profile**  
- Click **"View Profile"** at the top  
- Click **"Edit Profile"**  
- You can update your **name**, **email**, **password**, and **address** but **cannot change your ID/username**  
- Save the changes by clicking **"Save Profile"**  



### 🎥 3. **Browse Available Movies**  
- Click **"Home"** from the top navigation  
- Only movies with **available seats** (excluding upcoming movies) will be shown  
- Scroll through the list of movies available for booking  
- Click on a view movie to see more details about it  



### 🎭 4. **View Available Shows for a Movie**  
- Click on a movie from the available list  
- View all **available shows** for the movie  
  - **Show Details** include **date**, **time**, and **seat availability**  



### 🎟️ 5. **Book Tickets for a Movie**  
- After selecting a movie and showtime, click **"Book Tickets"**  
- Once confirmed, click on **purchase tickets**  



### 🎫 6. **View Your Booking History**  
- Click **"My Bookings"** from the top navigation  
- View all past and upcoming bookings  
- For each booking, you can see the details.



### ❌ 7. **Cancel a Ticket**  
- Go to **"My Bookings"**  
- Click **"Cancel"** for any booked ticket  
- After cancellation, the **seat availability** for the show will be updated accordingly, and the status will reflect **"Cancelled"**
